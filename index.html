About Me

With over a year of experience in web development, I have had the opportunity to lead a team of developers, manage projects directly with the final client through periodic meetings, and organize a key session to define the company's version control strategy.

In addition to my work experience, I have independently developed several projects aimed at providing solutions to real problems, which are currently being used.

I have a high level of English, which allows me to communicate fluently in international projects and work with clients and teams in multilingual environments.

Experience

At Kong Software, I worked as a full stack developer, taking on responsibilities from the start. They trusted me to lead a small team, which allowed me to apply my technical and management skills. I primarily worked with Angular, SQL, NodeJs, and Laravel, as well as style sheets like CSS.

I also had the opportunity to get involved in DevOps tasks, using Azure to implement CI/CD pipelines. My focus was on problem-solving, efficient development, and initial project design, always ensuring code quality and client satisfaction.

During the 2023-2024 academic year, I dedicated myself to providing personalized programming tutoring to several colleagues, helping them better understand concepts and improve their web application development skills. With regular sessions, I guided them to master the fundamentals and provided support to solve problems. Seeing them succeed in their web application development degree thanks to our joint work was very rewarding, demonstrating my ability to teach and my commitment to others' success.

Projects

Kiosk

The challenge was to create a web application that allows customers to place orders directly from a touch screen, connected to an ERP for centralized management.

Additionally, I integrated the functionality to connect to a ticket printer both via USB and network and the use of a card reader for direct payments from the kiosk itself.

Likewise, I had to design it to be adaptable for different types of restaurants, allowing customization through the ERP.

Security and Adaptability

I adapted the kiosk with protections against malicious actions, ensuring its reliability in a public environment. The project is deployed with CI/CD on Azure and offers online access for a demonstration.

I generated scripts for direct integration facilitating its mass incorporation.

Insurance Management Panel

Hover over to see the video

I developed an insurance management platform that allows creating insurances and products with dynamic forms, adding sub-products and annexes, and generating personalized certificates with the insurer's image and the broker's company.

The platform generates sales reports and allows commission management at lower levels. It includes a custom payment form with a payment gateway, and a user management system segmented by brokers and companies.

With integration to an ERP by company, it ensures centralized synchronization and is deployed with CI/CD on Azure for continuous updates.

Angular Laravel Azure SQL Server

Cinema Reservation Control

I developed a reservation control system for a cinema using the Model-View-Controller (MVC) design pattern in Angular. Users can check available sessions, view their active reservations, and cancel reservations.

Employees have access to an administration panel to manage all reservations, filter them, and cancel them as needed. Connected to a MySQL database, the system ensures data integrity and efficient reservation management.

Angular MySQL
